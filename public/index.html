SCORING SIGNALS

14 signals. One risk score. Zero guesswork.

Each signal adds points to the risk score. Higher score = higher risk = consider exiting. The score ranges from 0 (safe) to 22+ (critical). Points stack — a token can trigger multiple signals at once.

CRITICAL HIGH MEDIUM

CORE SIGNALS

#01CRITICAL

Liquidity

< $1,000 → +3 | < $10,000 → +2
Low liquidity = easy to rug. Below $1K is max danger zone.
#02HIGH

ATH Dump

> 50% from peak → +2 | > 30% → +1
How far price has dumped from all-time high. Big dump = holders trapped.
#03CRITICAL

Rug Ratio

> 0.5 → +3 | > 0.1 → +1
Ratio of insider sell pressure to buy pressure. High = rug in progress.
#04HIGH

Contract Renounced

NOT revoked → +2
Is contract ownership renounced? If not, dev can modify and rug.
#05CRITICAL

Top 10 Holders

> 80% supply → +2 | > 60% → +1
Concentrated holdings = massive dump risk when they sell.
#06HIGH

Volume 24h

< $5,000 → +2 | < $50,000 → +1
Dead volume means no exit liquidity. You won't be able to sell.

NEW IN V2

#07CRITICAL

Holders

< 10 → +2 | < 50 → +1
Few holders = no community, easy to manipulate price.
#08CRITICAL

Bot Rate

> 60% → +2 | > 40% → +1
Percentage of bot wallets. High = artificial volume and fake activity.
#09MEDIUM

Smart Money

0 detected → +1
Are experienced wallets in? If none, it's a red flag.
#10HIGH

Buy/Sell Ratio

< 0.5x → +1
More selling than buying = exit pressure building up.
#11HIGH

1h Price Change

< -20% → +2 | > +100% → +1
Sharp drops OR extreme pumps both signal abnormal risk.

DYOR-POWERED SIGNALS

#12HIGH

Contract Safety

Unverified → +2 | Proxy → +1
Is source code verified on-chain? Unverified contracts can hide malicious code.
#13CRITICAL

Token Age

< 1h → +3 | < 24h → +2 | < 7d → +1
Brand new tokens are highest risk. Most rugs happen within the first 24 hours.
#14HIGH

Vol/Liq Health

> 10x → +2 | > 5x → +1
Volume-to-liquidity ratio. Extreme ratios indicate wash trading or manipulation.
